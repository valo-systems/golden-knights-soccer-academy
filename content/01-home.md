# Home, Page Content Spec

| | |
|---|---|
| **Slug** | `/` |
| **In nav** | Yes (logo links here) |
| **Priority** | Phase 1 (launch) |
| **Primary audience** | All (sponsors, parents, community) |
| **Page goal** | Orient any visitor in seconds and route them to the right path: Join, Sponsor, or learn more. |
| **Primary CTAs** | "Join the Academy" (→ /register) · "Become a Sponsor" (→ /sponsors) |

---

## Section 1, Hero

**Purpose:** Instant identity + the two key actions.

**Contains:**
- Full-bleed background image (academy in action, training or match day) with a subtle dark/red overlay so text is readable.
- GKSA logo (white knight crest).
- Headline + one-line positioning statement.
- Two buttons: primary "Join the Academy", secondary "Become a Sponsor".

**Draft copy:**
- Headline: **Developing Midrand's footballers since 2016.**
- Sub: *Quality coaching, real opportunities, and a community that believes in every player.*

**Assets needed:** 1 high-quality wide hero image (landscape, min 1920px wide).

---

## Section 2, Impact strip

**Purpose:** Establish credibility instantly with hard numbers.

**Contains:** A row of 4 bold stat blocks.

| Stat | Label |
|---|---|
| 2016 | Founded |
| 100+ | Players developed |
| U9–U13+ | Age-group teams |
| SAFA | Registered (North Rand FA) |

**Notes:** Numbers animate counting up on scroll (nice-to-have). Keep claims accurate, confirm current squad ages.

---

## Section 3, Who we are (intro)

**Purpose:** A two-sentence summary that earns a click to the full story.

**Draft copy:**
> Golden Knights Soccer Academy is a youth football development academy in Midrand, Gauteng. We combine professional coaching with discipline, leadership, and life skills to develop well-rounded young athletes, on and off the field.

**CTA:** "Read our story" → /about

---

## Section 4, Programmes preview

**Purpose:** Send parents toward the right age group.

**Contains:** 3–4 cards (one per age band / programme) with an image, the age band, a one-line description, and a "Learn more" link → /programmes.

---

## Section 5, Sponsor band

**Purpose:** Make partnership visible and inviting on the home page itself.

**Contains:** A distinct red (`#EE3030`) full-width section with:
- Heading: **Partner with us**
- One line on impact + a row of current sponsor logos (sponsor wall preview).
- Button: "See sponsorship options" → /sponsors

**Draft copy:** *Back the next generation of Midrand football. Your brand on our kit, at our matches, and across our community.*

---

## Section 6, Latest news + gallery teaser

**Purpose:** Show the site is alive and active.

**Contains:**
- 3 most-recent news cards (image, title, date) → /news.
- A horizontal strip of 5–6 recent photos → /gallery.

---

## Section 7, Social proof

**Purpose:** Third-party credibility + live social.

**Contains:**
- Pull-quote from media coverage (Citizen / Midrand Reporter feature, or Soccer Laduma podcast spotlight).
- Embedded Instagram feed (@goldenknightsfc).

---

## Footer (global, appears on every page)

Logo · one-line mission · primary nav · contact details · social links · "Become a Sponsor" button · line: *Proudly developing youth football in Midrand since 2016.*
