# Programmes, Page Content Spec

| | |
|---|---|
| **Slug** | `/programmes` |
| **In nav** | Yes |
| **Priority** | Phase 1 (launch) |
| **Primary audience** | Parents & prospective players |
| **Page goal** | Answer "is this right for my child, and how do we join?" and drive registration. |
| **Primary CTA** | "Register / Book a trial" → /register |

---

## Section 1, Page hero

**Heading:** Our programmes
**Sub:** *Structured football development for every age, built on skill, discipline, and fun.*

---

## Section 2, Overview

**Draft copy:**
> At GKSA we develop more than footballers. Our programmes combine professional coaching with discipline, leadership, teamwork, and personal growth, so every player improves on the field and grows off it.

---

## Section 3, Age groups / teams

**Purpose:** Let a parent find their child's group instantly.

**Contains:** Card or table per age band. Confirm exact current bands with the head coach.

| Age group | Focus | Typical training |
|---|---|---|
| U9 | Fundamentals, coordination, love of the game | *confirm* |
| U11 | Core skills, positions, small-sided games | *confirm* |
| U13 | Tactical understanding, competitive play | *confirm* |
| U13+ / older | League & tournament performance | *confirm* |

**Note:** ⚠️ Fields marked *confirm* need the head coach to supply schedule, venue, and any fees.

---

## Section 4, What a typical week looks like

**Contains:** Training cadence (days/times), venue, what's provided vs. what to bring, match/tournament participation on weekends.

---

## Section 5, How to join

**Purpose:** Remove all friction from joining.

**Contains:**
- Steps: 1) Enquire / book a trial → 2) Attend a session → 3) Register.
- Fees / what's included (or "contact us for current fees" if not public).
- Financial assistance note, GKSA supports players who need help to participate.
- Prominent button: "Register / Book a trial" → /register.

---

## Section 6, Outreach programme

**Purpose:** Differentiator that matters to parents AND sponsors.

**Draft copy:**
> Through our outreach programme, GKSA supports children from surrounding communities, giving talented young players who face financial barriers the chance to train, compete, and belong.

**CTA (secondary):** "Support our outreach" → /sponsors
