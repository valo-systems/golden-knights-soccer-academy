# Sponsors & Partners, Page Content Spec ⭐ (centrepiece)

| | |
|---|---|
| **Slug** | `/sponsors` |
| **In nav** | Yes |
| **Priority** | Phase 1 (launch) |
| **Primary audience** | Sponsors (businesses, CSI managers, local entrepreneurs) |
| **Page goal** | Move a business from "interested" to "in touch", and make existing partners feel valued. |
| **Primary CTAs** | "Download the proposal (PDF)" · "Become a Sponsor" (enquiry form) |

This page is effectively a digital version of the academy's sponsorship proposal. It is the most important page on the site.

---

## Section 1, Hero

**Heading:** Partner with Golden Knights Soccer Academy
**Sub:** *More than sponsorship, an investment in youth development, community, and the future of South African football.*
**Buttons:** "Download the proposal" (PDF) · "Become a sponsor" (scrolls to form)

---

## Section 2, Why partner with GKSA

**Purpose:** Lead with the three benefits from the proposal.

**Contains:** 3 cards:
- **Brand visibility**, exposure across training, league matches, tournaments, community events, academy apparel, and digital platforms.
- **Marketing & engagement**, activations, events, tournaments, and social campaigns that connect your brand to the community.
- **Corporate Social Investment (CSI)**, measurable impact on youth development, education, sport, and community upliftment.

---

## Section 3, The numbers (impact)

**Contains:** Confident, scannable stat row:
- Founded 2016 · 100+ players · hundreds of family & community members reached · SAFA-registered (North Rand FA) · featured by The Citizen and Soccer Laduma.

---

## Section 4, What your sponsorship supports

**Draft copy:**
> Sponsorship funds go directly to quality training equipment, field rent, tournament registration fees, and support for players who need financial assistance to participate.

**In-kind items we welcome:** 2026/2027 kit (training + game), tracksuits, soccer balls & bags, training equipment (cones, bibs, vests, rings), first-aid kits, drawstring/portable bags, bottle carriers & bottles.

---

## Section 5, Sponsorship tiers

**Purpose:** The heart of the page, present as attractive cards, Platinum highlighted.

| Level | Contribution | Key benefits |
|---|---|---|
| **Platinum** | R100,000 | Largest logo on training & game kit; largest logo on gazebos, banners & bags; featured across all social media and the website. |
| **Golden** | R50,000 | Prominent logo on kit, gazebos and banners; featured on social media and the website. |
| **Silver** | R10,000 | Logo on kit, gazebos, banners, website and social media. |
| **Community Partner** | R5,000 | Logo on kit, website and social media. |
| **Custom / In-kind** | Flexible | Donate products, services or equipment, tailored recognition to match. |

Each card ends with a "Choose this level" button that pre-fills the enquiry form.

---

## Section 6, Sponsor wall

**Purpose:** Show partnership off, this is what makes sponsors feel valued.

**Contains:** Dedicated, well-designed grid of current sponsor logos, grouped/sized by tier (Platinum & Golden largest), each linking to the sponsor's website. Platinum/Golden also appear in the home-page sponsor band.

**Assets needed:** Current sponsor logos (SVG/PNG) + their URLs + tier.

---

## Section 7, Proof of delivery

**Purpose:** Show sponsors the visibility they get.

**Contains:** Photos of branded kit, banners, and gazebos at matches and events. Optional short testimonial from an existing partner.

---

## Section 8, Become a sponsor (conversion)

**Contains:**
- Short enquiry form: name, company, email, phone, interest (tier dropdown / in-kind), message.
- Big "Download the sponsorship proposal (PDF)" button, links to `docs/sponsorship-proposal.pdf`.
- Direct contacts: Head Coach Katlego Ntsheke (078 161 0670); Assistant Coach Mphatso (066 577 8001); PA Bophelo Monyayi (084 060 2704).

**Form routing:** submissions email the academy contacts; tier "Choose this level" buttons pre-select the dropdown.
