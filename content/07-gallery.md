## Section 2, Photo grid

**Contains:** Responsive masonry/grid with lightbox on click. Group into albums by season or event (e.g. "Match days", "Training", "Tournaments", "Community").

**Behaviour:** lazy-load images; compressed for fast mobile loading; alt text on each.

**Assets needed:** Curated, high-quality photos (the academy's existing match-day and team photos are a starting set; a fresh shoot would lift quality).

---

## Section 3, Video (optional)

**Contains:** Embedded highlight reels or training clips (YouTube/Instagram) where available.

## Section 4, Instagram feed

Embed @goldenknightsfc so the gallery stays fresh automatically and drives follows.
